cargo run

  Controls once the window opens:
  - Space — pause/resume simulation
  - Left mouse drag — orbit camera
  - Middle mouse drag — pan camera
  - Scroll wheel — zoom
  - Arrow keys — move J1/J2 joint targets
  - S — toggle spindle on/off
  - 1 — snap camera to workpiece
  - 2 — snap camera to robot arm
  - Esc — quit
